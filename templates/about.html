About FlightHub

Aviation Intelligence Platform

FlightHub is a comprehensive aviation intelligence platform that provides real-time flight tracking, weather data, and detailed information about airports, airlines, and aircraft worldwide.

Our Mission

At FlightHub, our mission is to democratize access to aviation data and make flight tracking accessible to everyone. Whether you're an aviation enthusiast, a frequent traveler, or a professional in the industry, we provide the tools and information you need.

We combine data from multiple reliable sources to give you the most accurate and up-to-date information about flights, weather conditions, and aircraft movements around the world.

Key Features

Everything you need for aviation intelligence

Real-Time Flight Tracking

Track flights in real-time with detailed information about departure, arrival, delays, and current status.

Live Aircraft Map

Visualize aircraft positions worldwide on an interactive map with real-time updates from OpenSky Network.

Airport Weather

Get current weather conditions for airports worldwide to plan your trips better and understand flight conditions.

Airport Directory

Browse detailed information about airports worldwide including location, timezone, and operational details.

Airline Database

Explore comprehensive airline information including fleet size, routes, and operational status.

Search History

Keep track of your searches and quickly access previously viewed flights and information.
